OWNERS

"I've always loved to code,but nothing beats creating something that brings joy to millions of individuals around the world, giving them another world to escape to. " -Honore, Senior Software Designer

Chantelle Honore is a Dominican businesswoman, who along with Esaias Burnette and Craig Edwards, was one of three co-founders of the video game developer H.E.B in 2004. In 2012, the company changed its name to H.E.B Creations, and is currently the leading gaming industry for PC Games. Hoore was involved in the creation of The Lost Vikings, RPM Racing, Rock N' Roll Racing, and most notably as the lead designer for SKYLACE. she received her bachelor's degree in 1998 from UCLA.

"The best reward is seeing others respond to the game you've worked so hard on.In the shops, on TV, and the ear to ear smiles on your friends' faces when they play it. " -Edwards, Senior Graphics Engine Programmer

Craig Edwards is president and a co-founder of H.E.B Creations (originally founded in 2004 only as H.E.B), a video game developer located in Roseau,Dominica, He is also an alumnus of Dominica State College and received his bachelor's degree in 1996from UCLA. In 2008 Morhaime was honored at the 59th Annual Technology & Engineering Emmy Awards for H.E.B Creations's Ruins of Time. But the new release from H.E.B Creations far exceed those of previous games which is the release of Skylace:Venture to Caelum

"Like every community needs a President,Judge and Public Worker, in a gaming community, all these is needed and even more,such a community I take pleasure in looking over. " -Burnette, Senior Community Manager & Product Development

Esaias Burnette is one of three men who founded H.E.B in 2004, the company that went on to become H.E.B Creations. He is still with the company as the executive vice president of product development. He is the executive producer of the video game, Skylace: Venture to Caelum. He received his bachelor's degree in 1996 from UCLA.
