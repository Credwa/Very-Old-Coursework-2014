What is Skylace?


Skylace is an online RPG game that takes flight beyond the old style adventure games. Unlike other RPG games, you can use your controller or keyboard and mouse to control the adventure like never before. With all the depth you expect from a traditional RPG game, plus the intense gratification of adventure, Skylace changes all the rules.
