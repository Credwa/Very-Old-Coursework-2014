Controls

Movement

Use the keys W A S D Q E to move.
W to move forward
S to move backwards
A to turn left
D to turn right
Q to strafe left
E to strafe right
Use Mouse to move camera


Basics

Use F to enter Skylacing mode [To Skylace(Flight mode)]
Use R to interact with various components
Use M to open up map
Mouse button 1 to attack
Mouse button 2 for secondary attack
Mouse button 3 to adjust camera
Keys 1~0 to use skills
use L to open up quest menu
use C to open Character menu


Social

Use G to open up guild menu
Use U to open up Friends list
Use Z to open up Alliance menu
Use P to open Party menu
Use B to open dungeon menu
